About MoodBot

Who we are and what we do


We at the team of Moodbot had the idea to be able to provide entertainment depending on your mood!

If you're feeling down just try out our Moodbot and see what is suggested for you.

Also, we wished to be able to fit into the schedules of all our users so we also implemented the time category.

If you either have 10 minutes or 2 hours, we have something in store for you.

You'll be able to select from a range of emotions such as happy, relaxed, and more.
